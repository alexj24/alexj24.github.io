My Dev Academy Learning Plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

I would love to have a career as web developer. I also think I'd be pretty cool to go overseas to somewhere like Australia or the United States and work for a big company, so that's something I'd like to achieve in the future at some point.

What are your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning?

  • Strength - Strategic. I tend to analyse things a lot before deciding which strategy is the best to go with
  • Strength - Trustworthy. I can be trusted I say I'm going to do a certain task then I make sure I do it
  • Strength - Empathetic. I try my best to be as considerate of others as I can
  • Limitation - Anxiety. Particularly with public speaking and presenting things in front of groups. I think I'll struggle with this and it will hold be back a bit.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

I think my biggest non-technical challenge will probably be speaking in front of the group since my anxiety really makes that a bigS struggle for me

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?

I'd like to see my teamwork skills developed during the course. Since I haven't coded with others besides myself yet.

What will I do to manage my workload throughout this course?

I will always try to work to the best of my ability and work efficiently so I'm not wasting others or my own time.

When things are building up and starting to get to me I like to think I'll try my best to keep calm and not get too stressed out. I'd also probably talk to my facilitator and other teachers for some help or advice.

How and where will I seek help?

Depending on what the problem I'm having is, I could seek help from different people. If it's a code related problem I can talk to others in my cohort or any of the teachers. If it's another problem or issue I have I can go talk to my facilitator or other Dev Academy people depending on the problem. How I would seek help would be by talking to them face-to-face or by messaging them on slack if I wasn't at campus.

What do I expect from the facilitation and bootcamp team

I expect them to help me achieve the best I can do throughout this course!

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things?

I don't really have any commitments that would get in the way of study during the day. But it is important to me to keep up my physical health, so I enjoy going to the gym or going for walks or biking. These things can always be done before or after study or on the weekends

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?

If I need stuck with something, whether it be technical or non-technical, I will go find someone that is able to help me with it. I will try to do this in a timely manner rather than being stuck for ages and not getting achieving any progress.

I wil manage my workload by working productively and staying on task. I wil also spend whatever time necessary of my own time to complete what I need to get done
